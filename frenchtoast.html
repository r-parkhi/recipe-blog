French Toast

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Cuisine: French

Serves 2, serving size: 2 slices

Total time: 40 mins



Ingredients








Instructions

  1. Bake bread for 10 min at 300°F in the oven/airfryer.
  2. Mix together JUST Egg, milk, sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. In a shallow dish, pour custard over bread, making sure to cover all sides. Soak for 8 minutes on each side.
  4. Melt butter on a skillet on medium heat.
  5. Pick up a slice of bread and let excess custard drip into the dish before placing it onto the skillet. Cook each slice for 2 mins on each side until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with maple syrup, powdered sugar, berries, or other toppings of choice!

Based on the recipe from the JUST Egg online cookbook!
