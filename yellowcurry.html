Yellow Curry

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Cuisine: Thai

Serves 3, serving size: 2 bowls

Total time: 25 mins



Ingredients







Instructions

  1. Cook rice as desired. Meanwhile, chop veggies and tofu.
  2. Bring a large pot to medium heat. Use a neutral oil to stir fry veggies from hardest to softest: carrot, potato, peas.
  3. In a small pan, stir fry tofu.
  4. Add sauce and tofu to pot, stirring slowly to combine.
  5. Let simmer for 1-2 mins. Serve warm over rice.
