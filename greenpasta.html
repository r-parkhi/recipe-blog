Green Pasta

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Cuisine: Italian

Serves 2, serving size: 2 bowls

Total time: 30 mins



Ingredients








Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in a pot according to directions on packaging, or until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large saucepan to medium heat. Add olive oil and minced garlic, watching closely so it does not burn. Cook until fragrant.
  3. Add zucchini and broccoli. Stir fry for 2-3 mins. Optional: use pasta water to lightly steam veggies.
  4. Drain pasta and add to the pan. Add pesto. Season with salt, pepper, & red crushed pepper to taste. Mix well until combined.
  5. Serve warm in a bowl with parmesan over top.
